FAQ

If your question is not answered below, feel free to get in touch.

Where is my test result data saved?

Locally on the machine where the test was performed. It is never transmitted over the network.

Can I change the test server?

Not as of the latest version.

What data does Speedster collect?

Speedster does not collect any user data. Take a look at the privacy policy for the details. The servers used for the test might keep anonymized data such as your public IP address.
